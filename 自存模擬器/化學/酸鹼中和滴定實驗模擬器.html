滴定曲線 (Titration Curve)

原理解釋： 初始溶液為強鹼 (NaOH)，含有高濃度的氫氧根離子 (OH-)，因此 pH 值極高。隨著加入強酸 (HCl)，酸中的氫離子 (H+) 會與 OH- 反應生成水 (H2O)。在達到等當點 (30 mL) 時，所有的 NaOH 皆被中和，溶液變為中性食鹽水，pH 值瞬間掉落至 7。繼續加入 HCl 會使溶液中充滿過量的 H+，導致 pH 值持續下降變為強酸性。 How it works: The initial solution is a strong base (NaOH) with a high concentration of hydroxide ions (OH-), so the pH is very high. As strong acid (HCl) is added, its hydrogen ions (H+) react with OH- to form water (H2O). At the equivalence point (30 mL), all the NaOH is neutralised, the solution becomes neutral salt water, and the pH drops sharply to 7. Adding more HCl floods the solution with excess H+, so the pH keeps falling and turns strongly acidic.
