數碼鴻溝Digital Divide

ICT · Access Gap

互聯網普及率並不平均。切換不同切入維度,看看誰被落下。Internet access is uneven. Switch the dimension to see who gets left behind.

最大差距widest gap
數碼鴻溝是指不同群體在接觸及運用資訊科技上的差距,成因包括經濟 (買不起裝置／上網)、地理 (偏遠地區缺基建)、年齡與教育 (數碼技能不足)。後果是資訊、教育、就業機會的不平等。縮窄鴻溝的方法:公共 Wi-Fi、資助裝置、數碼培訓等。(數字為示意教學數據。) The digital divide is the gap between groups in access to and use of ICT. Causes include economic (can\'t afford devices/data), geographic (no infrastructure in remote areas), and age / education (low digital skills). It creates inequality in information, education and jobs. Bridging it: public Wi-Fi, subsidised devices, digital training. (Figures are illustrative teaching data.)
