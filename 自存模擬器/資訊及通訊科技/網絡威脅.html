網絡威脅與防護Network Threats & Defenses

ICT · Security
① 選擇一種威脅① Pick a threat

② 開啟防護措施② Enable defenses
縱深防禦：沒有單一措施能擋所有威脅。防火牆過濾流量、防毒軟件偵測惡意程式、定期更新修補漏洞、備份對抗勒索軟件、WPA 保護 Wi-Fi、VPN 加密連線、用戶警覺防釣魚。組合使用才安全。 Defence in depth: no single measure stops every threat. A firewall filters traffic, antivirus detects malware, updates patch holes, backups beat ransomware, WPA secures Wi-Fi, a VPN encrypts the link, and user awareness stops phishing. Layer them for real security.
