奇偶檢驗加一個位元使 1 的總數為偶(或奇)。可偵測單一位元錯誤，但雙位元錯誤會互相抵消而偵測不到。檢查碼由前面數字經公式算出，附在末端；重算不符即代表輸入有誤 (常見於學生證、條碼、身分證)。 Parity adds a bit so the count of 1s is even (or odd). It catches single-bit errors, but a two-bit error cancels out and slips through. A check digit is computed from the leading digits and appended; if the recomputed value doesn't match, the input is wrong (used in student IDs, barcodes, HKID).
