SEQUENTIAL
  • ✓ 適合磁帶、簡單、順序讀取快✓ Good for tape; simple; fast for reading in order
  • ✗ 找特定記錄要逐筆掃描，慢✗ Must scan record-by-record to find one — slow
DIRECT
  • ✓ 由鍵值算出位址，直接跳到，快✓ Address computed from key — jump straight there, fast
  • ✗ 需要磁碟等可隨機存取的媒體✗ Needs random-access media (e.g. disk)
