FIG.06

檔案格式與壓縮File Formats & Compression

ICT · Multimedia Files
內容 / 品質Content / quality
無損壓縮 LosslessLossless
有損壓縮 LossyLossy
無損 (Lossless) 可完整還原原始資料 (如 png, wav, txt)。有損 (Lossy) 丟棄人眼/耳不易察覺的資訊以大幅縮小檔案 (如 jpg, mp3, mpeg4)，但品質無法百分百還原。相同內容，不同格式的大小差異可以很大。 Lossless restores the original data exactly (e.g. png, wav, txt). Lossy discards detail the eye/ear barely notices to shrink files hugely (e.g. jpg, mp3, mpeg4), but quality can't be fully recovered. The same content stored in different formats can differ greatly in size.
