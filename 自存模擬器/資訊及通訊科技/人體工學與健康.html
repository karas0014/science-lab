健康風險Health risks
人體工學研究如何設計工作環境以減少勞損。長時間不良姿勢與重複動作會引致:重複性勞損 (RSI)、腕管綜合症、眼睛疲勞、頸／背痛。要點:螢幕頂端與眼睛平齊、距離一臂、椅子承托腰背、雙腳平放、每 30–60 分鐘小休並望遠處 (20-20-20 法則)。 Ergonomics designs the workspace to reduce strain. Prolonged poor posture and repetitive motion cause RSI, carpal tunnel syndrome, eye strain, and neck/back pain. Keys: screen top at eye level, an arm\'s length away; chair supporting the lower back; feet flat; and a short break every 30–60 min looking far away (the 20-20-20 rule).
