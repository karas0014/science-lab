但怎知 Alice 的公鑰是真的？ — 證書機構 (CA) / PKIBut how do we trust Alice's public key? — Certificate Authority (CA) / PKI
🏛 CA (如香港郵政 e-Cert / HK PKI)(e.g. Hongkong Post e-Cert / HK PKI) 📜 數碼證書Digital Certificate 綁定「Alice ↔ 其公鑰」binds "Alice ↔ her public key"
數碼簽署反過來用鑰匙:Alice 用私鑰簽署文件的雜湊,任何人用她的公鑰驗證。若文件被改,雜湊不符 → 簽署無效,證明來源真確 (認證) 與完整性。CA 簽發數碼證書,擔保某公鑰確屬某人,構成公開密碼匙基礎建設 (PKI)。 A digital signature uses the keys the other way: Alice signs the document's hash with her private key; anyone verifies with her public key. If the document is altered, the hashes differ → invalid signature, proving authenticity and integrity. A CA issues digital certificates guaranteeing a public key really belongs to a person — this is Public Key Infrastructure (PKI).
