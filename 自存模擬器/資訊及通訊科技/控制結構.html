控制結構Control Structures

ICT · Seq / Sel / Iter
虛擬碼Pseudocode
執行狀態Execution state
任何程式都由三種控制結構組成:序列(逐行執行)、選擇(IF…THEN…ELSE 按條件分支)與迭代(WHILE／FOR 重複)。灰化刪線的行代表本次執行沒有走到的分支。 Every program is built from three control structures: sequence (run line by line), selection (IF…THEN…ELSE branches on a condition), and iteration (WHILE／FOR repeats). Greyed, struck-through lines are the branch not taken this run.
