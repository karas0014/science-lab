快取 (cache) 存放最近用過的資料。命中 (hit) 便極快;未命中 (miss) 要到較慢的 RAM 取，再載入快取 (取代最久沒用的)。由於程式常重複存取相近位址 (局部性)，快取能大幅加快平均速度。 Cache holds recently used data. A hit is very fast; a miss must fetch from slower RAM and load it into cache (evicting the least-recently-used). Because programs re-access nearby addresses (locality), the cache greatly speeds up the average access.
