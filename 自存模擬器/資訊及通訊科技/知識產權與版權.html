知識產權與版權Copyright & Creative Commons

ICT · CC Licence

你創作了一件作品,想以 Creative Commons (共享創意) 授權別人使用。選擇你的條件,看看組成哪一款授權。You made a work and want to license it via Creative Commons. Pick your terms and see which licence they form.

別人可以Others may
版權在作品完成時自動產生,毋須註冊。Creative Commons 讓創作者保留版權之餘,預先授予公眾部分權利。四個條件:BY 署名 (必選)、NC 非商業、ND 禁止改作、SA 相同方式共享。ND 與 SA 互相排斥。用別人有版權的作品前,先看清授權條款。 Copyright arises automatically when a work is created — no registration needed. Creative Commons lets creators keep copyright while granting some rights up front. Four terms: BY attribution (always required), NC non-commercial, ND no derivatives, SA share-alike. ND and SA are mutually exclusive. Always check the licence before reusing someone's work.
