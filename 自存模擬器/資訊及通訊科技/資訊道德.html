資訊道德Information Ethics

ICT · Ethics
道德分ethics 0

資訊道德關乎在數碼世界中作出負責任的抉擇 — 尊重知識產權、保護私隱、誠實不抄襲、不散播假資訊或作網絡欺凌。法律定下底線,道德要求更高。每個情境沒有「技術對錯」,而是問:這樣做對他人公平嗎?你願意公開承認嗎? Information ethics is about responsible choices in the digital world — respecting intellectual property, protecting privacy, being honest (no plagiarism), and not spreading misinformation or cyberbullying. Law sets the floor; ethics asks for more. Each scenario has no "technical" right answer — it asks: is this fair to others, and would you own it publicly?
