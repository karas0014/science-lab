☁ 互聯網/WANInternet/WAN
LAN (局域網) 覆蓋小範圍 (如一間學校),常以交換器組成星型;WAN (廣域網) 跨越大距離,把多個 LAN 經路由器連接 (互聯網即最大的 WAN)。每部裝置靠網絡介面卡 (NIC) 連網。 A LAN covers a small area (e.g. one school), often a star built around a switch; a WAN spans large distances, joining many LANs through routers (the internet is the biggest WAN). Every device connects via a NIC.
