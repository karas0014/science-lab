取樣率↑ = 每秒取更多樣本，波形更貼近原訊號。位元深度↑ = 更多量化階數，振幅更精細。兩者皆↑ → 品質更好但檔案更大。 Higher sample rate = more samples per second, closer to the analog wave. Higher bit depth = more quantisation levels, finer amplitude. Both ↑ → better quality but larger file.
