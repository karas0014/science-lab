DNS 像互聯網的電話簿。解析器由最頂層 (根) 開始,逐層被指引到管理該網域的權威伺服器,最終取得 IP。查得的結果會被快取一段時間 (TTL),下次更快。 DNS is the internet's phone book. The resolver starts at the top (root) and is referred down level by level to the authoritative server that owns the domain, finally getting the IP. Results are cached for a while (TTL) so the next lookup is faster.
