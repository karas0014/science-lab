最相關的香港法例是?Which HK law applies?
電腦罪行在香港受多條法例規管:未經授權存取／破壞電腦資料涉《刑事罪行條例》(如刑事毀壞、有不誠實意圖取用電腦);盜用信用卡或身分行騙屬《盜竊罪條例》的欺詐;濫用他人個人資料及「起底」違反《個人資料(私隱)條例》;下載或分發盜版則違反《版權條例》。 Computer crimes in Hong Kong fall under several ordinances: unauthorised access/damage to data engages the Crimes Ordinance (e.g. criminal damage, access to computer with dishonest intent); card/identity fraud is fraud under the Theft Ordinance; misusing personal data and doxxing breach the PDPO; downloading or sharing pirated works breaches the Copyright Ordinance.
