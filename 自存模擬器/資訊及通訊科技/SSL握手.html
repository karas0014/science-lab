SSL／HTTPS 握手SSL Handshake

ICT · HTTPS Security
💻 客戶端 BrowserClient (Browser) 伺服器 ServerServer 🗄
SSL/TLS 握手結合兩種加密:先用公鑰安全地交換一把對稱工作階段密鑰,之後所有資料用該對稱密鑰加密 (快)。伺服器的數碼證書證明其身分。完成後,網址列出現 🔒 (HTTPS)。 The SSL/TLS handshake combines both cryptography types: it uses public-key to safely exchange a symmetric session key, then encrypts all data with that fast symmetric key. The server's digital certificate proves its identity. When done, the address bar shows 🔒 (HTTPS).
