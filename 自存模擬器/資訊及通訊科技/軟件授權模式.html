授權 (licence) 訂明你可以怎樣使用軟件。專有軟件源碼保密、多數收費;共享軟件先試用後付費;免費軟件免費但不開源;自由／開源 (FOSS) 可自由使用、查看及修改源碼、再散布;公有領域則完全放棄版權。用了未獲授權的軟件 = 盜版,屬侵權。 A licence states how you may use software. Proprietary hides its source and usually costs money; shareware is try-before-you-buy; freeware is free but closed-source; free/open-source (FOSS) lets you use, read, modify and redistribute the source; public domain waives copyright entirely. Using software without a valid licence = piracy, an infringement.
