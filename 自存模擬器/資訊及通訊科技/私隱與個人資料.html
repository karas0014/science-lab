香港《個人資料(私隱)條例》六大保障原則HK PDPO — 6 Data Protection Principles
個人資料是任何可直接或間接辨識某人的資料。零碎資料經資料聚合 (data aggregation) 可拼出完整檔案,用於定向廣告甚至身分盜竊。香港《個人資料(私隱)條例》以六大原則保障個人資料 — 收集要有目的、要準確、要保安、當事人有查閱及改正權。分享前先想清楚。 Personal data is anything that can identify a person directly or indirectly. Scattered items, through data aggregation, assemble into a full profile used for targeted ads or even identity theft. Hong Kong's PDPO protects data through six principles — collection must have purpose, data must be accurate and secure, and you have rights of access and correction. Think before you share.
