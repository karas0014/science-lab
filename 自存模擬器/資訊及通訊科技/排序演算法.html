排序演算法：氣泡排序Bubble Sort

狀態State
氣泡排序反覆比較相鄰兩個元素,次序不對就交換,較大的值像氣泡般逐次「浮」到右端。每完成一輪,最右一個就固定 (變綠)。它簡單但慢,時間複雜度為 O(n²)。 Bubble sort repeatedly compares adjacent pairs and swaps them if out of order, so the largest value "bubbles" to the right each pass. After every pass the rightmost element is locked (turns green). Simple but slow — O(n²) time.
