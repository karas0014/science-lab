綠色貼士Green tips
綠色運算指以環保方式設計、製造、使用及棄置電腦。裝置耗電產生碳排放;過早更換及棄置則造成電子垃圾 — 內含鉛、水銀等有毒物,亂棄污染土壤及水源。做法:啟用省電模式、延長使用年期、維修而非更換,以及送到正規回收。(數字為示意教學估算。) Green computing means designing, making, using and disposing of computers in an eco-friendly way. Devices consume power → carbon emissions; frequent replacement and dumping create e-waste — containing toxic lead and mercury that pollute soil and water. Do: enable power-saving, extend device life, repair rather than replace, and use proper recycling. (Figures are illustrative estimates.)
