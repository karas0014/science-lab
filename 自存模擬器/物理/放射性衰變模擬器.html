物理筆記:Physics notes:

放射性衰變是隨機過程。未衰變數量 N 隨時間 t 呈指數下降：N = N0 e-kt。Radioactive decay is random. Undecayed nuclei N fall exponentially with time t: N = N0 e-kt.

在此模擬中，每次投擲代表一個時間單位。半衰期是指 N 降至 N0/2 所需的平均次數。Here each throw is one time unit; the half-life is the average number of throws for N to fall to N0/2.
