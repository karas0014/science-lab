物理概念 (DSE 重點)

根據德布羅意假說 (de Broglie Hypothesis)，電子具有波動性。當電子繞原子核運行時，它形成一個環形的物質波。

$$ 2\pi r = n\lambda $$

要形成穩定的軌道，圓周長度 ($2\pi r$) 必須是波長 ($\lambda$) 的整數倍 (n = 1, 2, 3...)，從而形成駐波 (Standing Wave)。若不為整數 (例如 n=3.5)，波會發生破壞性干涉而抵消，該軌道無法存在。

結合德布羅意波長公式 $\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$：

$$ 2\pi r = n \left( \frac{h}{p} \right) \implies pr = \frac{nh}{2\pi} $$

因為角動量 L = pr，由此推導出波耳的角動量量子化條件：

$$ L = n\frac{h}{2\pi} = n\hbar $$

According to the de Broglie Hypothesis, electrons exhibit wave-like properties. An orbiting electron forms a circular matter wave.

$$ 2\pi r = n\lambda $$

For a stable orbit, the circumference ($2\pi r$) must be an integer multiple of the wavelength ($\lambda$) (n = 1, 2, 3...), creating a Standing Wave. If non-integer (e.g., n=3.5), destructive interference occurs, and the orbit cannot exist.

Substituting de Broglie wavelength $\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$:

$$ 2\pi r = n \left( \frac{h}{p} \right) \implies pr = \frac{nh}{2\pi} $$

Since Angular Momentum L = pr, we derive Bohr's Quantization of Angular Momentum:

$$ L = n\frac{h}{2\pi} = n\hbar $$
